Goals for 2022

At the beginning of 2022 I decided to set some goals in order to keep myself accountable and to stretch myself. I have never been good with goals, nor do I consider myself to be still, but I am trying.

Goal 1

The first goal I had was to read a little bit of the scriptures in spanish every single day. I have a brother on a mission who is currently learning spanish, so I know that reading in spanish will help me not lose that, as well as just helping me keep up my spirituality.

Goal 2

My second goal was to wake up everyday before 8:30 am. Not going to lie this has been a hard one. I can say with 100% certainty that I have "woken up" before 8:30 am, but getting up before this time is a completely different story. I really want to master my morning, because I know that it will lead to increased productivity.

Goal 3

The third goal I had was to get a job. This is actually something that I have accomplished. I am currently working at Thread Wallets in their finance department. It has been a great experience. They are super great over there and they are very flexible with me working while also a full-time student.

Goal 4

The last goal that I had for this year was to get a 4.0 in my classes. Right now I am doing pretty well. I will have to update you at the end of the semester to let you know how it goes. My hardest class at the moment is Finance and I spend a lot of time on it every week. Hopefully I can put in the work to get the grades that I desire.
